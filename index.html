The Reward System

Two currencies,
one clear path to cash

⭐

XP Points

Earned by playing battles. XP has no cash value — it's your rank in the community and part of your withdrawal eligibility.

🪙

Cash Coins

1,000 Cash Coins = $1.00 USD. Earned only by completing offers in the Earn tab. Withdraw when eligible.

⚠️ Fair Play Rules

Withdrawals require KYC verification, a minimum battle history, sufficient XP, and fraud review. We maintain a fair environment for everyone.

How the two currencies work

  • ✓
    Play battles to earn XP — Correct answers earn XP, wrong answers deduct XP. XP builds your rank and counts toward withdrawal eligibility. XP has no cash value.
  • ✓
    Complete offers to earn Cash Coins — Cash Coins are earned only by completing sponsored surveys and offers in the Earn tab. This is the only way to earn withdrawable value.
  • ✓
    Watch rewarded ads for gameplay perks — Optional ads unlock extra battles and restore lifelines. Ads do not earn Cash Coins.
  • ✓
    Refer friends for Cash Coins — Earn 500 Cash Coins per verified referral. Share your code with friends.
  • ✓
    Withdraw when eligible — Once you meet the XP, battle, and KYC requirements, cash out via Payoneer or USDT.
