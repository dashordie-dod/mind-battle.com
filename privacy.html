3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To operate the quiz battle gameplay and XP system
  • To credit and process Cash Coin earnings and withdrawals
  • To verify your identity before payouts
  • To detect and prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse
  • To serve rewarded video advertisements
  • To operate the referral programme
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset, withdrawal status)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising

MindBattle uses Google AdMob to serve rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect your Advertising ID and device information. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Offerwall

When you engage with offerwall offers, you interact with a third-party offerwall network and their offer providers. MindBattle shares your user ID and offer completion status with the offerwall network to credit your earnings. We do not share your name, email, or KYC documents with offerwall providers.

4.3 Cloud Services

We use industry-standard cloud infrastructure (including Google Firebase) to store and process your data securely. Data is stored on servers with appropriate security standards.

4.4 Payment Processors (Payoneer & USDT)

When you request a withdrawal, we share the information required to process your payout with the relevant payment provider. For Payoneer withdrawals, we share your Payoneer email and withdrawal amount with Payoneer (see Payoneer's Privacy Policy). For USDT withdrawals, your payout is sent to the crypto wallet address you provide on the blockchain network you select; blockchain transactions are public and irreversible by nature.

5. Data Security

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Access controls ensure users can only read and write their own data
  • Sensitive account fields are protected server-side and cannot be modified directly by users
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections
  • KYC documents are reviewed securely and not shared with unauthorised parties

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware.

7. Children's Privacy

MindBattle is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us at support@mind-battle.com.

8. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data (see Account Deletion Policy)
  • Objection — object to processing of your data for direct marketing
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format

Contact us at support@mind-battle.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. International Data Transfers

MindBattle is operated from the United States, and your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence. By using MindBattle, you consent to such transfers, which are protected by industry-standard security measures.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via in-app notice at least 14 days before taking effect.
