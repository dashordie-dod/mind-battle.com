Everything you need to know about MindBattle

Getting Started
MindBattle is an educational quiz battle app where you compete in knowledge challenges across categories like Science, History, Sports, Geography, and General Knowledge. You earn XP by playing and can earn real cash by completing offerwall offers inside the app.
MindBattle is available to users aged 18 and above. You must register with a valid email address. KYC (identity verification) is required before any cash withdrawal.
Download MindBattle, tap "Register", enter your email, choose a username, and set a password. You can also sign in with Google. Your account is created instantly.
Gameplay
Before a battle, choose your category. Each battle has 50 questions across 5 rounds with a timer per question.
  • Correct answer: +2 XP
  • Wrong answer: −1 XP
  • Skipped / timed out: 0 XP
  • Net XP per battle is never negative
You get 5 free battles per day. After that, tap "Watch Ad to Play" to unlock additional battles by watching a short rewarded video. Free battles reset daily at midnight UTC.
Lifelines help you during a battle:
  • 50/50 — removes two wrong answers
  • Skip — skips the current question with no penalty
  • +Time — adds extra seconds to your timer
Watch a rewarded ad during a battle to restore all lifelines.
If you press the home button or switch apps mid-battle, the current question is automatically recorded as incorrect and the battle moves on. This ensures fair play for all players.
MindBattle currently includes Science, History, Geography, Sports, and General Knowledge. You choose your category before each battle. More categories will be added over time.
XP & Rewards
XP (Experience Points) is your in-game progression currency. It has no cash value and cannot be withdrawn. XP determines your rank tier and leaderboard position, and is one of the requirements to unlock the withdrawal feature.
XP is purely for status and competition. It is not money and cannot be exchanged for money.
Cash Coins are the redeemable currency in MindBattle. They are earned only by completing offerwall offers — not by playing battles and not by watching ads.
Fixed ratio: 1,000 Cash Coins = $1.00 USD
No. Battles earn XP only — XP has no cash value. Watching rewarded ads unlocks gameplay benefits (extra battles, lifelines) — not Cash Coins. Cash Coins come exclusively from completing offerwall offers.
Withdrawals & Payouts
All requirements must be met simultaneously. Full details are shown in the Wallet tab of the app. Requirements include:
  • Verified email address
  • Approved KYC identity verification
  • Sufficient lifetime XP earned through gameplay
  • Account age requirement met
  • Minimum battles played
  • Active login streak
  • No penalty flags
  • Minimum balance of $50.00
  • Minimum per withdrawal: $50.00
  • Maximum per withdrawal: $100.00
  • Payout method: Payoneer only
  • Processing time: 3–10 business days after approval
KYC (Know Your Customer) is identity verification required before any cash payout. You will need to submit a valid government-issued ID. Contact kyc@mind-battle.com to start the KYC process.
Referral Programme
Share your unique referral code (found on your Profile screen) with friends. When a referred friend meets all verification criteria, you earn Cash Coins per verified referral. Full details including payout thresholds are shown in the Profile section of the app.
The referred user must meet all verification criteria, which include playing a minimum number of battles, earning sufficient XP, having a minimum account age, completing KYC, and having no penalty flags. Self-referral and fake accounts result in permanent ban.
